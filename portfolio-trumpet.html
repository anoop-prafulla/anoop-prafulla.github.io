The Trumpet Website

ROLE: UX & UI / TYPOGRAPHY

INSTRUCTOR: ELISA ARDENI

PROJECT BRIEF

The Trumpet is a editorial fashion e-commerce website inspired from Net-A-Porter. The goal of the project is to integrate editorial style magazine into an online format. The play in typography hierarchy and rendering typefaces for the web was exhibited in this piece. Exploration of a 12 column grid system in relation to web typography rules is communicated.

QUESTIONS

Creation of a strong visual identity for the luxury e-commerce The Trumpet.

METHODOLOGY

“The Trumpet” is a luxury e- commerce website. Not the “usual” luxury brand: it’s for bold people that want to shout out their bright personality to the world. “the Trumpet” is the musical, strong voice they need
