I'm a Hyderbad based Indian Designer & Art Director

As a young digital dreamer influenced by technology, design and people, always lean towards simplicity and human touch. Comprehensive user-centered design process from user research to beautiful pixels.

I approach the Design using minimal and refined aesthetics my goal is to leave enough space for the actual story. Strong concepts then become even stronger, simply executed in effective and functional way. By reducing the noise and visual clutter along the process, suddenly every detail becomes important.

My diverse design backgrounds shape me as a hybrid designer who uses design thinking and storytelling to solve the world’s most wicked problems and cement long-term social impact.
